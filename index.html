161 Collective

Julian Smith-McLallen

Julian Smith-McLallen is a keyboardist and producer from Philadelphia, Pennsylvania. His style is heavily influenced by jazz and brazilian music, however he has branched out into other styles including fusion, electronic, hip-hop, and RnB. Now, he is one half of the 161 collective with Trumpet player Forrest Aubrey. Together, they create exploratory, futuristic music and sound that draws from a wide range of influences.

Forrest Aubrey

Forrest is a saxophonist from Seattle, Washington. He began playing in elementary school band. In college he later picked up the EWI, which he developed his playing of under the tutelage of Darren Barrett. He wants to push the boundaries of electronic jazz and noise music, and his favorite musicians are Theo Croker and M.K.Gee.
